What is my favorite video game?

The video game that I like the most is Genshin Impact. Maybe some of you are asking, "why you like Genshin Impact?". I have three reason why i like Genshin Impact.



The first reason is for it's relaxing gameplay. It is undeniable that I, as a student, don't have much time to play games. Therefore, games that are relaxed and can be played in my spare time in a short time are what I really need. The second reason is for the story. The story of Genshin Impact has a plot that is quite complicated and deep. Especially from how the main character tries to find his twin. The last reason is the eye-pleasing graphics. Even though the artstyle applied is of a cartoonish type. However, the graphics and shading provided are relatively good and above the average of other cross-platform games.
